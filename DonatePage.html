Thank you for helping community cats by supporting Spay Our Strays!

SOS is an all-volunteer 501(c)(3) organization. We depend on your support to help community cats, including spay/neuter, vet care for illness/injury, and food for managed colonies. We welcome monetary and in-kind donations!

Amazon Smile

Did you know Amazon donates money to charitable organizations through their AmazonSmile program? You simply shop as you normally would and Amazon donates on your behalf!

Simply go to https://smile.amazon.com  and select Spay Our Strays as your charitable organization! We receive 0.5% of all eligible purchases.
